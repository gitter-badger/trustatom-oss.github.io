Trustatom Opensource

At Trustatom, we help people to harness the potential of smart contracts and the blockchain technology. We provide a next generation multi-factor authentication and privacy-oriented identity technology, Identity & Contract APIs, as well as some niche applications.

Trustatom could not have existed without those vast amounts of open source. Our products stay on the shoulders of giants. We are happy to have an opportunity to contribute, both to new and existing projects. We'll be soon publishing some of our work on GitHub.

Bounties

We use cutting edge technologies and stacks. They have much better chance at survival and proliferation if there is enough of quality libraries available. We build our extended open source team through bounties. Check them out!

Bounty 1: Bitcoin Elliptic Curve for Elixir (suggested amount: 3 BTC)

There's some basic ECDSA support in Erlang's crypto module. But that's not enough to sign Bitcoin transactions. As an example, there's a fairly good implementation in Go with a decent test coverage.

Bounty 2: BIP32 support for Elixir (suggested amount: 2 BTC)

Deterministic wallets. As an example, there's a fairly good implementation in Go with a decent test coverage.

Bounty 3: ECIES support for CoreBitcoin (suggested amount: 3 BTC)

ECIES encryption scheme for CoreBitcoin (Objective-C). As an example, there's an implementation in JavaScript.
